Lawful bases and data protection rights

Under UK data protection law, we must have a lawful basis for collecting and using your personal information.

Your data protection rights include access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and the right to withdraw consent.

Where we get personal information from

How long we keep information

Retention Period
We operate on a zero-retention basis. When you delete your account, we immediately delete your profile, CVs, applications, and associated data from our database and storage. Google Firebase may retain encrypted backups for up to 7 days for disaster recovery purposes, after which they are automatically removed.
